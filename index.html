About Us

Craftsmanship & Quality

At Vaughans Upholstery, we believe that quality craftsmanship is the foundation of exceptional upholstery. With years of experience and a passion for detail, we take pride in transforming tired furniture into stunning pieces that enhance your space.

Our approach combines traditional upholstery techniques with modern materials to deliver results that are both beautiful and durable. Whether you're looking to restore a family heirloom or refresh a contemporary piece, our skilled team ensures every project receives the attention it deserves.

We work closely with each client to understand their vision and provide personalized solutions that exceed expectations. From fabric selection to the final touches, we're committed to quality at every step of the process.
