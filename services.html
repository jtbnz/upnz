What We Offer

At Vaughans Upholstery, we provide a comprehensive range of upholstery services to meet all your needs. Whether you're looking to restore a family heirloom, refresh your home furniture, or outfit a commercial space, our skilled craftsmen deliver exceptional results with attention to detail and quality materials.

Residential Upholstery

Transform your home furniture with our expert residential upholstery services. We work with a wide range of furniture pieces, from sofas and armchairs to ottomans and dining chairs, bringing new life to your favorite items.

Our residential upholstery service includes a consultation to understand your vision, fabric selection assistance, and meticulous craftsmanship to ensure your furniture not only looks beautiful but provides lasting comfort.

What We Upholster:

  • Sofas and sectionals
  • Armchairs and recliners
  • Dining chairs
  • Ottomans and footstools
  • Headboards and bed frames
  • Window seats and cushions
  • Antique and vintage furniture

Our Process

We follow a thorough process to ensure every upholstery project meets our high standards of quality and craftsmanship. Here's how we transform your furniture:

1

Consultation

We begin with a detailed consultation to understand your needs, preferences, and vision for your furniture.

2

Assessment

Our experts examine your furniture to assess its condition and determine the best approach for upholstery.

3

Material Selection

We help you select the perfect fabrics, leathers, or other materials that match your style and durability needs.

4

Quotation

We provide a detailed quote outlining the scope of work, materials, timeline, and cost for your project.

5

Craftsmanship

Our skilled upholsterers carefully strip, repair, and rebuild your furniture with meticulous attention to detail.

6

Quality Check

Every piece undergoes a thorough quality inspection to ensure it meets our high standards.

7

Delivery

We carefully deliver your beautifully upholstered furniture back to your home or business.

8

Aftercare

We provide care instructions and ongoing support to help maintain your upholstery for years to come.
