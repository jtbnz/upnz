Frequently Asked Questions

How much does upholstery typically cost?

Upholstery costs vary depending on the size of the piece, the complexity of the work required, and the materials chosen. We provide detailed quotes after assessing your furniture, ensuring transparency in our pricing.

How long does an upholstery project take?

Project timelines depend on the complexity of the work and our current schedule. Simple projects may take 1-2 weeks, while more complex restorations can take 3-4 weeks. We'll provide a specific timeline when quoting your project.

Do you provide fabric selection assistance?

Yes! We offer expert guidance on fabric selection, helping you choose materials that match your style preferences, durability needs, and budget. We can provide samples and recommendations based on your specific requirements.

Can you restore antique furniture?

Absolutely. We specialize in antique restoration, using traditional techniques and appropriate materials to preserve the character and value of your antique pieces while ensuring they're functional and beautiful.

Do you offer pickup and delivery services?

Yes, we offer pickup and delivery services within the Auckland area. For larger items or customers outside our standard delivery area, we can arrange special transportation at an additional cost.
