About Us

Craftsmanship, quality, and attention to detail

Our Story

At Vaughans Upholstery, we believe that quality craftsmanship is the foundation of exceptional upholstery. With years of experience and a passion for detail, we take pride in transforming tired furniture into stunning pieces that enhance your space.

Craftsmanship & Tradition

Vaughans Upholstery was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to provide exceptional upholstery services that combine traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques. What began as a small workshop has grown into a respected upholstery business serving residential and commercial clients throughout Auckland.

Our founder, Vaughan, learned the art of upholstery through years of apprenticeship and practice. His dedication to quality and attention to detail forms the cornerstone of our business philosophy. Today, our team of skilled craftspeople continues this tradition, bringing expertise and passion to every project we undertake.

We believe that furniture tells a story, and our work is about preserving and enhancing that story. Whether we're restoring a family heirloom or creating custom pieces for a commercial space, we approach each project with the same level of care and commitment to excellence.

Our Journey

2010

The Beginning

Vaughans Upholstery was founded as a small workshop focusing on residential upholstery services.

2013

Expanding Services

New Workshop

Today
